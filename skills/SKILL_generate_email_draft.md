## Description: Generate email draft for human review (draft-only, no API calls).

## Prompt:
Using filesystem tools only, read files from vault_path='AI_Employee_Vault/Needs_Action'. Look for email-related content or requests to send emails. Generate email drafts for human review and approval.

### Step 1: Identify Email Requests

Scan all .md files in /Needs_Action for:
- `type: email` in frontmatter
- Email indicators: 'send email', 'send an email', 'email to', 'reply to', 'compose email'
- Email patterns: email addresses, subject lines, To:/From: fields

### Step 2: Extract Email Details

For each email request, extract:
- **To:** Recipient email address
- **CC:** Optional CC recipients
- **Subject:** Email subject line
- **Body:** Email content/body
- **Priority:** From frontmatter or content (urgent, high, normal)

### Step 3: Generate Email Draft

Create draft file in /Pending_Approval: Email_Draft_{datetime.now().strftime('%Y-%m-%d_%H-%M-%S')}.md

Draft format:
```markdown
---
type: email_draft
status: pending_review
generated_at: {ISO timestamp}
source_file: {triggering file name}
---

## Email Details

**To:** {recipient email}
**CC:** {cc email or N/A}
**Subject:** {subject line}
**Priority:** {priority level}

## Email Body

{Full email body content}

---

## Approval Actions

- [ ] Approve (send email manually via Gmail/Outlook)
- [ ] Reject (add your reason below)
- [ ] Edit (add your changes/notes below)

---
*Draft generated by SKILL_generate_email_draft.md - No API calls made*
```

### Step 4: Handle Natural Language Requests

For natural language requests like "Send email to john@example.com about the project update":
- Parse the request to extract to/subject/body
- Generate professional email content
- Create draft with extracted details

Example input:
```
"Send email to client@company.com with subject 'Invoice Follow-up' and body 'Reminder about overdue invoice #12345'"
```

Example output draft:
```markdown
---
type: email_draft
status: pending_review
generated_at: 2026-02-22T10:30:00
---

## Email Details

**To:** client@company.com
**Subject:** Invoice Follow-up

## Email Body

Dear Client,

This is a friendly reminder regarding overdue invoice #12345.

Please process the payment at your earliest convenience.

Best regards

---

## Approval Actions

- [ ] Approve (send email manually via Gmail/Outlook)
- [ ] Reject (add your reason below)
- [ ] Edit (add your changes/notes below)
```

### Step 5: Log Draft Creation

Append to /Dashboard.md:
```markdown
## Email Drafts Created - {timestamp}

| To | Subject | Status |
|----|---------|--------|
| {email} | {subject} | Pending Approval |
```

**CONSTRAINTS:**
- Do NOT call MCP email endpoints
- Do NOT send emails automatically
- Do NOT access Gmail API
- Output ONLY the draft file content
- No additions, no hallucinations
